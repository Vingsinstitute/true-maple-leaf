Show answer

2. How many provinces and territories does Canada have?

Show answer

3. Canada is the second-largest country in the world by area. Which is largest?

Show answer

4. Which ocean borders Canada to the west?

Show answer
The Pacific Ocean.

5. Which Great Lake is entirely within Canada?

Show answer
None of them. Lake Ontario, Erie, Huron and Superior are all shared with the U.S. (Only smaller lakes like Lake Winnipeg are fully Canadian.)

6. What is Canada's smallest province by area?
