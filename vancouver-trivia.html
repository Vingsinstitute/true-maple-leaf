Landmarks & geography

1. Is Vancouver the capital of British Columbia?

Show answer
No, Victoria is. Vancouver is BC's largest city.

2. How big is Stanley Park?

Show answer
About 405 hectares, roughly 1,001 acres, larger than New York's Central Park.

3. Which ocean does Vancouver sit on?

Show answer
The Pacific Ocean.

4. What is the name of the mountain with a gondola overlooking downtown Vancouver from the North Shore?

Show answer
Grouse Mountain.

5. Who is Vancouver named after?

Show answer
Captain George Vancouver, a British explorer.

6. What is the name of the suspension bridge, a major North Shore tourist attraction, that crosses a canyon in North Vancouver?

Show answer
Capilano Suspension Bridge.

Sports & the Olympics

7. In what year did Vancouver host the Winter Olympics?

Show answer
2010.

8. What NHL team calls Vancouver home?

Show answer
The Vancouver Canucks.

9. Have the Canucks ever won the Stanley Cup?

Show answer
No, they have reached the Final three times but never won.

10. What sailing venue and mountain resort co-hosted alpine events during the 2010 Games?

Show answer
Whistler.

Culture & city

11. Which Vancouver-born actor plays Deadpool?

Show answer
Ryan Reynolds.

12. What is the approximate population of the city of Vancouver proper?

Show answer
Roughly 700,000 to 750,000 people (recent estimates), with over 2.7 million in the wider Metro Vancouver region.

13. What is the name of Vancouver's distinctive sail-shaped convention centre and cruise terminal on the waterfront?

Show answer
Canada Place.

14. Vancouver is home to one of North America's largest neighbourhoods of which kind, centred around Pender and Main Streets?

Show answer
Chinatown.

15. What popular seawall activity loops around Stanley Park along the water?

Show answer
Walking, running or cycling the Stanley Park Seawall.

16. Which major Canadian film and TV industry hub has earned Vancouver the nickname "Hollywood North"?

Show answer
Its film and television production industry.

17. What mountain range rises directly north of the city, visible from downtown?

Show answer
The North Shore Mountains, part of the Coast Mountains.

18. Vancouver is the largest city in which Canadian province?

Show answer
British Columbia.
