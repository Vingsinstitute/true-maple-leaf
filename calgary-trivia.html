Landmarks & geography

1. Is Calgary the capital of Alberta?

Show answer
No, Edmonton is. Calgary is Alberta's largest city.

2. How tall is the Calgary Tower?

Show answer
About 190.8 metres, roughly 626 feet.

3. In what year did the Calgary Tower open?

Show answer
1968 (it was called the Husky Tower at first, renamed Calgary Tower in 1971).

4. Which two rivers meet in downtown Calgary?

Show answer
The Bow River and the Elbow River.

5. What mountain range is Calgary often called the "gateway" to?

Show answer
The Canadian Rocky Mountains.

6. What is the distinctive shape of the Scotiabank Saddledome's roof?

Show answer
A saddle shape, built for the 1988 Winter Olympics.

Olympics & sports

7. In what year did Calgary host the Winter Olympics?

Show answer
1988.

8. What NHL team plays at the Scotiabank Saddledome?

Show answer
The Calgary Flames.

9. In what year did the Calgary Flames win the Stanley Cup?

Show answer
1989.

10. What CFL team calls Calgary home?

Show answer
The Calgary Stampeders.

Culture & city

11. What is Calgary's famous 10-day summer festival, nicknamed "The Greatest Outdoor Show on Earth"?

Show answer
The Calgary Stampede.

12. In what year did the Calgary Stampede first take place?

Show answer
1912.

13. What is Calgary's approximate population, making it Alberta's largest city?

Show answer
Roughly 1.5 to 1.6 million people (2025 estimate), for the city proper.

14. What major industry has earned Calgary the nickname "Canada's energy capital"?

Show answer
The oil and gas industry.

15. What annual event sees the Stampede grounds fill with rodeo events, chuckwagon races and a midway?

Show answer
The Calgary Stampede.

16. What is Calgary's light rail transit system called?

Show answer
The CTrain.

17. Which mountain resort town, a popular day trip from Calgary, sits inside Banff National Park?

Show answer
Banff.

18. Calgary is the largest city in which Canadian province?

Show answer
Alberta.
